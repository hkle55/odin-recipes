Homemade Naruto Ichiraku Ramen

I grew up on the show naruto and everytime I would see the main character eat ramen it always made my mouth water just by how good it looks So as a ramen enjoyer I looked up a recipe and decided to copy it and share it here.

Ingredients !!!

  • 2 pkg. top ramen
  • 5 cups pork (or beef broth, You can use the stuff in a box, the stuff in a can, or the cubes or the paste)
  • Pork Tenderloin
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Baby Bok Choy
  • 1 green onion
  • soy sauce
